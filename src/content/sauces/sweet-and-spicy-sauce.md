## Ingredients

- `2 tbsp` maple syrup
- `2 tbsp` olive oil
- `1/2 tbsp` Sriracha
- `1/2 tbsp` tamari or soy sauce
- `1/2 tsp` black pepper

## Instructions

Add all ingredients to a bowl and whisk. Add more Sriracha for more spice, up to `2 tbsp`.

Goes well with roasted cauliflower.
